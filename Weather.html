About: This was my first assignment where I had to use a fetch call in JavaScript. Here we used a prexisting api from OpenWeatherMap to tell the weather in the United States based off a zipcode input.

Challenges: At the time I was not aware that the hyperlink used for the
fetch request could be customized. While I did the CSS styling and had
the weather actually change based off a location, the problem was how
it was reading the input. Orginally it was not reading zipcodes and
instead read the name of cities in Europe. Once I realized that Apis
are customizable I was able to change it to the region of the United
States and can now be searched based off of input.

Accomplishments: I was able to complete an Asignment where people
could actually make use of something that is practical and easy to use.
You just have to type your preferred zipcode and the weather will change
accordingly. Also a neat feature is that if the Weather goes over 90
degrees or under 40, the temperature will change to red or blue respectively.
